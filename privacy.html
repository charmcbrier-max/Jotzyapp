1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — your email address and password when you create an account
  • Audio recordings — voice recordings you create in the app to generate notes
  • Notes and study content — text, flashcards, quizzes, and other content you create or save
  • Support communications — any messages you send to support@jotzyapp.com

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — features used, session length, errors, and interaction patterns
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Purchase information — subscription and payment data processed through Apple and RevenueCat (we never store your payment card details)

2. How we use your information

  • Provide, operate, and improve JotzyAI and its features
  • Transcribe your audio recordings into text using OpenAI's Whisper API
  • Generate study notes, flashcards, and quizzes from your transcripts using AI
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important notices about the app (updates, security alerts)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • OpenAI — audio data is sent to OpenAI's API for transcription. Per OpenAI's API terms, this data is not used to train their models.
  • Supabase — we use Supabase to store your notes, account data, and app content securely.
  • RevenueCat — we use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases.
  • Apple — purchases and payments are processed through the Apple App Store.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business transfers — if McBrier Events, LLC is involved in a merger or sale, your information may be transferred. You will be notified of any such change.

4. Data retention

  • Audio recordings are not stored permanently after transcription is complete.
  • Notes and study content are retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • Account information is retained until you delete your account.

You may delete your content or account at any time by contacting support@jotzyapp.com.

5. Data security

We use industry-standard protections via Supabase to secure your data in transit and at rest. No system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

6. Children's privacy

JotzyAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, contact us at support@jotzyapp.com and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a portable copy of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@jotzyapp.com with the subject line "Privacy Request."

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when we do. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of JotzyAI after changes means you accept the updated policy.
